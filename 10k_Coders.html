Overview

10000 Coders is an innovative educational institution dedicated to equipping individuals with the skills required to secure high-paying software jobs. Founded in 2018, the school offers a 7-month immersive program designed to meet the latest hiring needs in the tech industry.

Mission and Program

The primary mission of 10000 Coders is to transform unskilled individuals into skilled learners who can become highly valued engineers. The structured curriculum is tailored to accelerate students' careers by teaching them to build professional-level applications. This hands-on approach ensures that graduates possess the foundational knowledge needed to develop production-ready applications and demonstrate their ability to add real value to potential employers.

Company's USP (Unique Selling Proposition)

At 10K Coders, our USP lies in our unwavering commitment to student success. We understand that entering the tech industry can be daunting, especially for individuals without prior experience. Therefore, we have structured our program with the following key features:

  1. Outcome-Based Education: Our curriculum is meticulously designed to meet the latest hiring needs in the tech industry. We focus on practical, hands-on learning experiences that equip students with the skills demanded by employers.
  2. Personalized Support: Each student at 10K Coders receives personalized support from experienced instructors and mentors. We understand that every learner is unique, and we tailor our guidance to meet individual needs, ensuring that every student has the resources they need to succeed.
  3. Community Engagement: Beyond the classroom, 10K Coders fosters a vibrant community of learners. Students have the opportunity to collaborate with peers, participate in hackathons and coding competitions, and network with industry professionals, creating valuable connections that can accelerate their career growth.
